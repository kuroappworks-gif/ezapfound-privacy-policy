Privacy Policy for ezapfound

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application ezapfound (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by KURO APP WORKS. The Application is a simple home launcher designed to help users organize installed applications and create quick-access shortcuts for essential contacts.

1. User-Provided Information

The Application does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers. However, the Application allows you to manually enter the following information, which is stored locally on your device only:

  • Contact Shortcut Information: Names and phone numbers provided by the user for the purpose of creating SOS/Contact shortcuts. This data is used solely to enable the "one-tap dial" functionality.

2. Automatically Collected Information / Non-Personal Data

The Application collects and stores the following data locally on your device for the sole purpose of providing the core functionality of the launcher:

  • Installed App Information: Package names, application labels, and installation times of apps installed on your device.
  • User Settings and Preferences: Configuration data such as category names, app organization/sorting order, theme/font size settings, and background image URI.
  • Backup Data: If you use the Backup feature, a JSON file containing your categories and app organization is created on your device's storage at your direction.

None of the above locally stored data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties, except as required by the third-party services listed below (Section 4).

3. Permissions Used and Their Purpose

The Application requires the following permissions, used only for the purpose described:

  • android.permission.INTERNET: Required for the display of advertisements and consent management.
  • com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID: Required by Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob) for advertising and analytics purposes.
  • android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO: Used only when the user voluntarily taps the voice search/mic icon. Audio data is processed by the device's native speech recognition service and is not recorded or stored by this Application.

4. Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party service providers:

  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements.
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK: For managing user consent for personalized advertising (GDPR/CCPA compliance).

These services may collect information for the purposes of serving personalized ads, frequency capping, and ad reporting. Please refer to Google’s privacy policy for more details.

5. Data Retention Policy

All locally generated data (app lists, categories, settings) is stored on the user's device until the Application is uninstalled or the user manually deletes the data. Please note that backup files created by the user may remain on the device's storage after uninstallation and must be deleted manually.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Application is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
