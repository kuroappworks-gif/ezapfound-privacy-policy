Privacy Policy for ezapfound

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application ezapfound (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by KURO APP WORKS. The Application is a simple home launcher designed to help users organize installed applications into categories for easy access.

1. User-Provided Information

The Application does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information from its users. "Personal Information" includes, but is not limited to, name, address, email address, phone number, or geographic location.

2. Automatically Collected Information / Non-Personal Data

The Application collects and stores the following non-personal data *locally on your device* for the sole purpose of providing the core functionality of the launcher:

  • Installed App Information: Package names, application labels (display names), and installation times of apps installed on your device. This data is used solely for the function of the Application (displaying, searching, and organizing your apps).
  • User Settings and Preferences: Configuration data such as category names, app organization/sorting order, theme/font size settings, and background image URI.

None of the above locally stored data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties, except as required by the third-party services listed below (Section 4).

3. Permissions Used and Their Purpose

The Application requires the following permissions, used only for the purpose described:

  • android.permission.INTERNET: Required for the display of advertisements (via AdMob).
  • com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID: Required by Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob) for advertising and analytics purposes.
  • android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO: Used only when the user voluntarily taps the voice search/mic icon to enable speech-to-text functionality for app searching. The audio data is processed by the device's native speech recognition service and is not recorded or stored by this Application.

4. Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party service provider:

  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements. AdMob may collect information for the purposes of serving personalized ads, frequency capping, and ad reporting. Please refer to Google’s privacy policy for more details on their data collection practices.

5. Data Retention Policy

All locally generated data (app lists, categories, settings) is stored on the user's device until the Application is uninstalled or the user manually deletes the data.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Application is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should contact us.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes.
